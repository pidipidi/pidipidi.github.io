Biography

Daehyung Park is a Ph.D. candidate in the College of Computing and the Institute for Robotics and Intelligent Machines at the Georgia Institute of Technology, working with Dr. Charles C. Kemp in the Healthcare Robotics Laboratory. He was a robot engineer at the Mechatronics Center of Samsung Electronics Inc. He is currently researching assistive manipulation and multimodal execution monitoring methods for people with disabilities.
